About

Hi! I'm Ilyass Sofi, a game programmer. I mostly focus on Gameplay, User Interface, Artificial Intelligence and Tools.

I have always been fond of the gaming industry since I grew up playing, so in my late teens I started chasing my dreams.

Making games is my passion and I'm glad I found people that shared it, making me collaborate with multiple teams and create wonderful experiences.

Education

Born in Morocco and raised in Spain, learned Arabic and Spanish at an early age. Studied in a French school (LFM) which made me study French and English as well as focus more on my passion, programming.

Went on to study engineering in Brest, France, then came back to Madrid and got my Game Development degree specialized in Game Programming at ESNE.

I also have particularly invested time in some aspects of game developement aside from mine such as concept art, 3D modelling, special effects, audio design and more
